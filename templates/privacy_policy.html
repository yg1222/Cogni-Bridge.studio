Privacy Policy

We may collect personal information about you when you interact with our website or use our Services. The types of personal information we may collect include:

  • Account Information: such as your username, password, and other authentication credentials.

  • Communication Information: such as correspondence and feedback you provide to us.
  • Conversion Information: such as account IDs, user IDs, and necessary document or block IDs for converting monday.com documents into monday boards using OpenAI's API.
  • Other Information: any other information you voluntarily provide to us.

We may use the information we collect for various purposes, including to:

  • Improve our website and Services.

We may share your personal information with third parties in the following circumstances:

  • For Legal Purposes: We may disclose your information when required by law or in response to a valid legal request.

4. Data Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously. Personal identifiable information, such as names and email addresses, is never stored in plain text. Instead, we use industry-standard encryption methods to protect this data both in transit and at rest. Sensitive information, including authentication credentials, is securely stored using encryption techniques that prevent unauthorized access.

In addition to encryption, we implement rigorous screening of all incoming requests to protect against various threats, including SQL injection, OS injection, and other forms of cyberattacks. Our security protocols ensure that data handling processes are compliant with industry best practices, safeguarding the integrity of our systems.

While we implement reasonable measures to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. Therefore, while we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

You have choices regarding the collection, use, and sharing of your personal information. You may choose not to provide certain information, but this may limit your ability to access certain features of our website and Services.
